Privacy Policy

FocusGuard
Last Updated: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the FocusGuard mobile application ("App"), a screen time management app that helps you reduce phone usage through walking quests.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve your experience:

  • Health Data (Step Count): With your explicit permission, we access your step count data from Apple HealthKit to track your walking quest progress. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to external servers.
  • Screen Time Data: With your permission via Apple's Family Controls framework, we access information about which apps you choose to block. App selection tokens are stored locally on your device using App Groups.
  • Quest & Usage Data: Your quest progress, streak counts, unlock history, and focus scores are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Apple App Store. We do not store your payment information.
  • Device Information: Technical information such as device type and operating system version may be collected to improve app performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Track your daily walking quests and step progress
  • Manage app blocking and unlocking based on quest completion
  • Display your focus score, streaks, and statistics
  • Process in-app purchases through Adapty
  • Send local notifications for quest reminders and achievements
  • Improve the App's performance and user experience

On-Device Processing

FocusGuard is designed with privacy at its core. Your health data (step counts) and screen time data are processed entirely on your device. We do not transmit this data to any external servers. All quest progress, statistics, and app preferences are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms (SwiftData, UserDefaults with App Groups). There is no cloud synchronization or remote data storage for your personal data.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Adapty: For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions. Privacy Policy
  • Apple HealthKit: For reading step count data with your permission. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Family Controls / Screen Time: For managing app blocking with your permission.

Advertising

FocusGuard does not display any third-party advertisements. We do not collect or share advertising identifiers (IDFA).

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Your health and screen time data never leaves your device.
  • Purchase data may be shared with Adapty solely for the purpose of managing subscriptions.
  • We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Retention

  • All personal data is stored locally on your device and is retained as long as the App is installed.
  • Uninstalling the App permanently removes all locally stored data.
  • Subscription status data managed by Adapty is subject to Adapty's data retention policies.

Children's Privacy

FocusGuard is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the App or provide any personal information.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, it benefits from Apple's built-in device security measures, including device encryption and biometric protection. We do not transmit sensitive data over the internet.

If you reside in Brazil, you have the following rights under the LGPD:
