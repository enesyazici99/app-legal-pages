Privacy Policy

Memorium — A Museum of Memory
Last Updated: May 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Memorium mobile application ("App").

Our Privacy Commitment

Memorium is a single-player puzzle game that runs primarily on your device. We do not collect or store personal information on our servers. Your gameplay progress, settings, and preferences live locally on your device.

Information We Collect

  • Gameplay Progress: Levels completed, scores, and exhibit reveal state are stored locally using on-device storage (Hive). This data never leaves your device.
  • Settings: Audio preferences, daily challenge state, and similar in-app settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Apple's App Store. We rely on RevenueCat to verify your subscription status; RevenueCat receives an anonymous user identifier and your subscription state. We do not receive your payment details.
  • Advertising Identifier: If you grant App Tracking Transparency permission, our ad partner may receive your IDFA for ad personalization. You can decline this prompt or change your choice at any time in your device's Settings.

Third-Party Services

Memorium uses a small number of third-party services to deliver content, show ads (free tier), and manage subscriptions:

  • AppLovin MAX (advertising): Serves interstitial and rewarded ads on the free tier. AppLovin and its mediation partners may collect device identifiers and ad-engagement data subject to your ATT permission. See AppLovin's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat (subscription management): Manages in-app purchase state and entitlements. Stores an anonymous user identifier. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: Processes in-app purchases. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • jsDelivr CDN: Serves the exhibit reveal photographs and intro videos. jsDelivr does not require accounts and may log standard request metadata (IP address, request time) for caching. See jsDelivr's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting): Captures anonymized crash logs so we can fix bugs. No personal information is associated with the reports. See Firebase's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

  • Save and resume your progress through the museum's exhibits
  • Cache reveal photographs and intro videos so they don't re-download every session
  • Show ads (free tier) and process subscription purchases
  • Diagnose crashes and improve stability

Data Storage

All gameplay data — including progress, settings, scores, and downloaded media — is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App removes this data.

Advertising

Memorium offers a free tier that shows interstitial and rewarded ads served via AppLovin MAX. Subscribers to the "No Ads" or higher tier do not see any advertising, and no ad SDKs are initialized for those users on launch.

If you grant App Tracking Transparency permission, ad partners may use your advertising identifier (IDFA) for personalized ads. If you deny ATT, you will still see ads, but they will be less personalized.

In-App Purchases

Memorium offers optional subscriptions and one-time purchases:

  • No Ads (weekly / one-time): Removes all advertising and grants unlimited peeks.
  • Curator's Pass / No Ads + 3 Modes (weekly / one-time): Above plus access to the three premium game modes.

Subscriptions auto-renew until cancelled in your Apple ID settings. Purchases can be restored on the same Apple ID.

Children's Privacy

Memorium is not directed at children under 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us so we can address it.

Your Privacy Rights by Region

GDPR (European Economic Area & UK)

If you reside in the EEA or UK, you have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data, and to withdraw consent for ad personalization at any time via your device's ATT settings.

LGPD (Brazil)

If you reside in Brazil, you have rights of access, correction, deletion, and consent withdrawal under the LGPD. Contact us to exercise them.

CCPA (California)

We do not sell your personal information. If you reside in California, you may exercise your CCPA rights by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will reflect any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
