Frequently Asked Questions

How does Rootly work?

Rootly uses AI to generate personalized educational content organized as visual knowledge trees. Choose a topic you want to learn about, and Rootly creates an interactive tree of interconnected concepts. Explore each node to dive deeper into subtopics and build your understanding.

What is a knowledge tree?

A knowledge tree is a visual map of a topic, where each branch and node represents a related concept or subtopic. Starting from a root topic, the tree expands into more specific areas, helping you see how ideas connect and progressively deepen your learning.

How do daily limits work?

Free users have a daily limit on the number of new nodes they can generate. This limit resets every day. Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited node generation and access to all features without daily restrictions.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the app settings and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previous subscriptions linked to your Apple ID or Google account. If you're still having trouble, make sure you're signed in with the same account you used for the original purchase.

How do I change the content language?

You can change the content language in the app settings. Rootly supports multiple languages for AI-generated content. Select your preferred language, and new content will be generated in that language.

How do I report incorrect content?

Since Rootly uses AI to generate content, occasional inaccuracies may occur. If you find incorrect information, please email us with the topic, the specific node or content in question, and what you believe is incorrect. We take content accuracy seriously and will review your report.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the app settings. Tap on your profile, then select "Delete Account". This will permanently remove your account and all associated data, including your learning progress, knowledge trees, and preferences. This action cannot be undone.

The app is crashing or I found a bug. What should I do?

Try these steps: 1) Make sure you have the latest version of the app, 2) Restart your device, 3) Reinstall the app. If the issue persists, please contact us with your device model, iOS/Android version, and a description of the problem. Screenshots or screen recordings are very helpful!
