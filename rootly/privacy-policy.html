Privacy Policy

Rootly
Last Updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Rootly mobile application ("App"), an AI-powered learning app with visual knowledge trees.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve your learning experience:

  • Account Information: Your email address and authentication data when you create an account or sign in.
  • Learning Progress Data: Your knowledge trees, nodes completed, streak data, and content preferences are stored in our cloud database to sync your progress across devices.
  • Usage Analytics: Information about how you interact with the App, including features used, session duration, and learning patterns.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store your payment information.
  • Device Information: Technical information such as device type, operating system version, and language settings may be collected to improve app performance.
  • Crash Logs & Performance Data: Anonymous technical data may be collected to improve app performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save and sync your learning progress across devices
  • Generate personalized AI-powered educational content based on your learning preferences
  • Process in-app purchases through Adapty
  • Track your learning streaks and achievements
  • Improve the App's performance and user experience
  • Communicate with you about your account or the App

AI-Generated Content

Rootly uses artificial intelligence to create educational content, including knowledge tree topics, learning nodes, and explanations. This content is generated dynamically based on your selected topics and learning preferences. Your interactions with AI-generated content may be used to improve the quality and relevance of future content.

Data Storage

Your account data and learning progress are stored securely in the cloud using Supabase, a cloud database platform. This enables you to access your data across multiple devices. Supabase implements industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For cloud data storage and user authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Adapty: For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions. Privacy Policy

Advertising

Rootly does not display any third-party advertisements. We do not collect or share advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID).

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Your data may be shared with our third-party service providers (Supabase, Adapty) solely for the purpose of operating the App.
  • We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Retention

  • Your account data and learning progress are retained as long as your account is active.
  • If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
  • Anonymous analytics data may be retained for up to 12 months.

Children's Privacy

Rootly is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the App or provide any personal information.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Privacy Rights by Region

GDPR (European Economic Area & UK)

If you reside in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification of your data
  • Right to erasure of your data
  • Right to object to data processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

CCPA (California)

If you reside in California, you have the following rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected about you
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

LGPD (Brazil)

If you reside in Brazil, you have the following rights under the LGPD:

  • Right to know whether your personal data is being processed
  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct incomplete or outdated data
  • Right to delete anonymized or unnecessary data
  • Right to withdraw your consent
